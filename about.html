About Black Raven Logistics

Black Raven Logistics is a realism-focused logistics organisation built on disciplined operations, professional conduct, and structured execution across multiple operational environments.

One command structure • Three specialist divisions • Single Discord application path
Join Discord (Apply) View Divisions

Mission

To deliver structured, realism-driven logistics operations with disciplined standards of conduct, reliable coordination, and clear accountability across all divisions.

Core Principles

  • Discipline: Follow chain of command and maintain operational control at all times.
  • Professionalism: Clear communications, respectful conduct, and reliable participation.
  • Realism: Authentic standards appropriate to each operational environment.
  • Consistency: Standardised setups, documented procedures, repeatable results.
  • Accountability: Role responsibilities, tracked performance, enforceable rules.

Chain of Command

  • Owner: Strategic direction, final authority, organisation-wide decisions.
  • Operations Manager: Operational control, standards enforcement, division management.
  • Senior: Discipline support, trusted operational presence.
  • Driver/Pilot: Verified operator, participates in official operations.
  • Trial: Probationary operator under evaluation before verification.
